## Metric

We measure **Glyph**'s performance in **OCR** and **HTR** tasks using the **Character Level Accuracy** (%), computed using the **Levenshtein** distance. The following formula is employed: 

$$
ACC (\%) = 100 * \Bigl[1 - min\Bigl(1, \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{i=N}{Levenshtein(\hat{text}, text)}}{\sum_{i=1}^{i=N}{|text|}}  \Bigr)\Bigr]
$$

where $$\hat{text}$$ represents the recognized text and $$text$$ is the ground truth.

## Datasets

For benchmarking, we mainly use scanned documents provided by our collaborators which most often include ground truth information and results generated by similar services (e.g., FineReader, Transkribus, Tesseract, etc.). This helps us provide objective performance metrics and comparisons between Glyph and other alternatives.

We split the provided datasets according to **language** and **font type**.
